Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Zodiac Hype Poster does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users beyond what is necessary for TikTok API authentication.

2. How We Use Information

The Application uses TikTok OAuth credentials solely to post content to your authorized TikTok account. No data is shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored securely and only used for posting content. Generated zodiac images and captions are temporarily processed and not permanently stored.

4. TikTok Integration

By connecting your TikTok account, you authorize the Application to post content on your behalf. You can revoke this access at any time through TikTok's app settings.

5. Cookies

This Application does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with TikTok API, Google Drive, and Google Sheets. Please review their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your authentication credentials.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

9. Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact us via GitHub.
